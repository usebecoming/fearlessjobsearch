Refund & Cancellation Policy

Subscriptions

All subscription plans (Starter, Pro, Unlimited) are billed monthly or annually and can be cancelled at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again after that date. We do not offer refunds for the current billing period, partial months, or unused searches.

To cancel, go to Account Settings and select "Manage Subscription." You can also email us at support@lfyconsulting.com and we will cancel on your behalf within 24 hours.

Coaching Sessions

Sessions with Dr. Benjamin Ritter are booked and paid in advance at $500 per session.

  • No refunds or cancellations once a session is booked
  • Rescheduling is available with at least 48 hours notice at no charge
  • No rescheduling for requests within 48 hours of your session or no-shows
  • If Dr. Ritter needs to cancel, you will receive a full refund or the option to reschedule, your choice

To reschedule, email support@lfyconsulting.com with your booking confirmation at least 48 hours before your session.

Privacy Policy

What We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Resume content you upload or paste into the platform
  • Job search criteria and preferences you enter
  • Jobs you save, select, or favorite
  • Payment information (processed by Stripe. We do not store card details)
  • Basic usage data (pages visited, features used)

How We Use It

  • To generate AI-powered job matches, contact suggestions, and outreach sequences
  • To save your pipeline and search profiles across sessions
  • To improve the relevance of future searches within your account (your favorited and selected jobs inform your next search. Your data is never used to train AI models for other users)
  • To process payments and send booking confirmations
  • To send transactional emails (receipts, session confirmations, product updates)

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your resume or career data to train AI models beyond your own personalized experience
  • We do not share your information with employers, recruiters, or any third party without your consent

Third-Party Services

Fearless Job Search uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic — AI model provider (your search data is sent to Anthropic's API to generate results; subject to Anthropic's usage policies)
  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Vercel — hosting
  • Cloudflare — domain and CDN

Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing support@lfyconsulting.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Your Rights

If you are located in California (CCPA) or the European Union (GDPR), you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@lfyconsulting.com to make a request.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.
