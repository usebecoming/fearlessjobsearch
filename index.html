How it works

From search criteria to outreach-ready in under 5 minutes.

01

Target Your Search

Tell us what you're going after. Drop in your target titles, locations, and salary range. Upload your resume and we'll score every opportunity against your actual experience. Add specific companies and we'll surface contacts there whether or not they're actively hiring.

02

See What's Out There

Real listings from LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, and ZipRecruiter — filtered for relevance, scored against your resume, and ranked by fit. No staffing agencies. No noise. Just the roles worth your time, with an AI explanation of why each one matches your background.

03

Get to the Right People

For every role you select, we surface up to 10 verified contacts at that company — the hiring manager, internal recruiters, and the executives one level above the role. Real LinkedIn profiles. Current employment confirmed. No guessing, no cold searching.

04

Start the Conversation

Get a personalized outreach sequence for each contact — written from your resume, in your voice, at peer level. Four messages across 14 days covering the connection request, follow-up, re-engage, and final touchpoint. Copy, paste, send. You do the connecting. We do the writing.

The Result

Land Interviews, Not Applications

Track every conversation in your pipeline. See who you've reached out to, who's responded, and where you stand. You're not sending resumes into the void anymore. You're having real conversations with the people who actually make hiring decisions.
